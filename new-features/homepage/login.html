Evolution

The DevOps Evolution

From manual operations to AI-powered autonomous delivery — three generations of software engineering.

Generation 1

DevOps

CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure automation, containers, monitoring. Manual decisions and human-triggered deployments.

Generation 2

DevSecOps

Security integrated into every pipeline stage. Shift-left testing, compliance as code, GHAS scanning, zero-trust by default.

Generation 3

Agentic DevOps

AI agents that observe, reason, and act autonomously. Multi-agent orchestration, self-healing systems, intelligent deployment decisions.
